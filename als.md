 # The ALS Mission

## Why ALS?

ALS (Amyotrophic Lateral Sclerosis) is a progressive neurological disease that affects nerve cells responsible for voluntary muscle movement.

Over time, individuals living with ALS lose the ability to walk, speak, eat and eventually breathe independently.

Currently there is no cure.
